About Us

Professional food machinery manufacturer since 2008

ANHUI JIN KE FOODSTUFF MACHINERY CO., LTD is a modern food machinery manufacturing enterprise that integrates production, research & development, and sales. With intelligent mechatronics products and continuous innovation, we have become one of the most large-scale professional bakery & pancake equipment manufacturers in China and abroad.

Our products meet GMP requirements and are widely used in the production of tortillas, flatbread, pies, buns and local specialty foods. The equipment is sold all over China and exported to the Middle East, Southeast Asia, Europe, the United States and many other regions.
