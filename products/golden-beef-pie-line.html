Technical Parameters

Supported Products Golden beef pie, military pancake, court beef pie, guokui-style variants
Core Modules Dividing, sheeting/forming, filling (optional), shaping, conveying
Forming Method Industrial forming with adjustable size/thickness (by configuration)
Automation PLC control; sensor monitoring; safety interlocks and alarms
Integration Connects to baking, cooling, stacking, packaging (optional)
Customization Product format, weight range, capacity target, layout planning

Process Flow

  1. Dough preparation
  2. Dough dividing / portioning
  3. Sheeting / shaping base
  4. Filling feeding (if applicable)
  5. Forming / pressing / final shaping
  6. Discharge to baking line
  7. Cooling / stacking / packaging (optional)

Options & Customization

  • Multi-product conversion kits (by product variant)
  • Different size/thickness/weight ranges
  • Layout according to factory space and utilities
  • Integration with ovens, coolers and packaging lines
